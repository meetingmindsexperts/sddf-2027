About the Forum

Where the Region's Best Minds Meet Digestive Health

The Saudi Digestive Diseases Forum is the Kingdom's premier gathering for gastroenterologists, hepatologists, and GI surgeons — designed to translate cutting-edge science into clinical practice across the GCC.

Hosted by the Saudi Gastroenterology Association, SDDF 2027 brings together regional and international faculty for two intensive days of plenary sessions, case discussions, workshops, and industry symposia.

Learn More

Scientific Excellence

All sessions peer-reviewed under the guidance of a multidisciplinary scientific committee representing the Kingdom's leading GI institutions.

CME Accreditation

SDDF 2027 will carry full CME accreditation — recognised continuing education credits applicable across GCC licensing bodies.

Industry Engagement

A curated exhibition and satellite symposia programme connects delegates with the latest innovations in GI therapeutics, devices, and diagnostics.

Scientific Themes

Key Focus Areas for 2027

Sessions structured around the most pressing clinical challenges and emerging therapeutic frontiers in digestive health across the GCC.

01

IBD & Advanced Biologics

Optimising treat-to-target strategies, biosimilars, and novel small molecules in inflammatory bowel disease management.

02

Hepatology & MASH

From NAFLD to MASH — new diagnostic pathways, pharmacotherapy pipelines, and the role of GLP-1 agents in liver disease.

03

Advanced Endoscopy

AI-assisted colonoscopy, third-space endoscopy, and ESD/EMR — live demonstrations and hands-on skills transfer.

04

GI Oncology

Colorectal cancer screening gaps in KSA, early-onset CRC trends, and multidisciplinary management pathways.

05

Microbiome & Functional GI

FMT protocols, precision probiotics, and the evolving science of the gut-brain axis in clinical practice.

06

Paediatric Gastroenterology

Paediatric IBD, growth optimisation, and building transition-of-care models across GCC healthcare systems.
