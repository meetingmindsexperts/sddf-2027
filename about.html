About the Forum

About SDDF 2027

The vision, mission, and people behind the Kingdom's leading digestive diseases congress.

Our Vision

The Saudi Digestive Diseases Forum was established to create a world-class platform where the Kingdom's gastroenterology and hepatology community gathers annually to exchange knowledge, challenge conventions, and advance patient care standards across Saudi Arabia and the broader GCC.

SDDF 2027 marks a landmark edition of the forum — expanding its scope to encompass the full spectrum of digestive diseases, from inflammatory bowel disease and hepatology through to advanced endoscopy and GI oncology.

The Saudi Gastroenterology Association

SDDF 2027 is hosted by the Saudi Gastroenterology Association (SGA), the professional body representing gastroenterologists and hepatologists across the Kingdom. The SGA is committed to advancing medical education, supporting research, and shaping healthcare policy in digestive diseases at the national and regional level.

The Association works in close collaboration with the Saudi Commission for Health Specialties (SCFHS) and international societies including the World Gastroenterology Organisation (WGO) and the European Society of Gastrointestinal Endoscopy (ESGE).

Why Attend?

SDDF 2027 offers an unmatched opportunity to engage with leading regional and international faculty across two days of peer-reviewed scientific content. Whether you are a consultant gastroenterologist, a fellow in training, a GI surgeon, or a hepatologist — the forum is designed to deliver directly applicable learning across every subspecialty.

Delegates will leave with updated clinical knowledge, new professional connections, and full CME credit documentation for SCFHS and other GCC licensing bodies.

Congress Management

SDDF 2027 is professionally managed by Meeting Minds Experts (MM Group), a GCC-based specialist healthcare events and medical communications agency with over a decade of experience delivering landmark medical congresses across the region.
