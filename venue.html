About Jeddah

Jeddah is Saudi Arabia's second largest city, a major Red Sea port, and an international hub for commerce, culture, and healthcare. King Abdulaziz International Airport connects Jeddah directly to over 100 destinations worldwide, with major hubs including Dubai, Doha, London, Frankfurt, and Singapore.

The city offers world-class accommodation, dining, and hospitality. November is one of the most pleasant months to visit, with mild temperatures averaging 27–32°C.
